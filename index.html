Lesson 1

Assemble Computer Hardware

This lesson covers the proper assembly of computer hardware, including safety measures. It highlights key components such as the motherboard, processor, RAM, power supply, storage devices, and input/output devices, ensuring compatibility and functionality. The step-by-step process involves installing the motherboard, CPU, RAM, and storage, connecting the power supply and peripherals, and securing cables for a stable system.

Preparing Installer

Introduction

Welcome to the topic “Install and Configure Computer Systems.” This learning material provides essential content and activities to help you develop the skills needed for assembling computer hardware, preparing an installer, installing an operating system and drivers for peripherals/devices, installing WinRAR application software, and conducting testing and documentation. You will also learn the importance of following safety protocols, ensuring hardware and software compatibility, and troubleshooting installation issues to maintain a fully functional and optimized computer system. This lesson includes reading materials, hands-on activities, and assessments to measure your understanding. Completing these lessons and activities is required to successfully master the installation and configuration process.
